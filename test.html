WHO ARE WE ?

EcoVista is your friendly eco-buddy on the web! It's the go-to place for real-time environmental insights, like air quality and carbon footprint. But here's the cool part—EcoVista is also all about recycling. Users can scan batteries, earn points, and be recycling superheroes. The platform is not just informative; it's a hub for eco-campaigns, eco-friendly products, and inspiring stories from the community about saving the planet. Oh, and by helping the government track household batteries, EcoVista is on a mission to reduce e-waste. It's not just a website; it's EcoVista—everyone's guide to a chill, green lifestyle!
