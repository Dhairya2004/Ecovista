What is
EcoVista ?

EcoVista is the urban dweller's guide to smart living, offering real-time environmental insights, battery recycling gamification, and eco-friendly marketplace. By tracking household batteries, it aids government & promotes safe disposal & recycling .

  • Real-time Environmental Insights on our user-interactive map.
  • Scan old batteries, earn and redeem exclusive rewards, climb that leaderboard.
  • Explore eco-friendly products, Join campaigns and much more.
  • Helping government to track household batteries & promote greener urban development.
